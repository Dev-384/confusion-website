Unleash Your Creativity

Welcome to Confusion, a unique space dedicated to the exploration of bizarre and innovative programming ideas. Here, we believe that the most extraordinary ideas often seem bizarre at first glance.

The Idea

We get it. Sudden inspiration, no real place to store your ideas. It's a struggle to find a place where you can just gush all your ideas out.

The idea is to provide a platform where developers can share their most creative and seemingly bizarre programming ideas, and keep them all together in a neat and organized fashion.

Features

Projects of any size, Confusion is there to help.

  • Project Management

    Manage multiple files and pages for your project with ease


  • Documentation

    Keep your projects well-documented with our integrated documentation tools


  • Combating AI

    No one want's to be replaced. We don't want that either. In hopes to combat this, we plan on making deals with "the big AI comanies" to NOT use your ideas and code to train the machines.


  • Creativity Unleashed

    With Confusion, there aren't many restrictions. Let your imagination run wild!
    If you're curious about the restrictions there are, see: Our Restrictions

Restrictions

The vision for Confusion is still a work in progress.

Our idea is here, and we are trying to find ways to make it work.

Some of the common restrictions we expect to face will be:

  1. Server Uptime

    We want your code to be accessable to you, no matter what.
    This could be importing JavaScript modules hosted by us, or images

  2. Rule Enforcing

    People do yucky things, and we don't want any of that monkey buissness.

What is this place?

Have you ever had an idea, but no where to put it? What about multiple projects, stored with no organization? THIS is a place for those kinds of things. Random projects, keeped nice and tidy!

How It Works

Confusion is flexible.
Each project is assigned one markdown file, and a director to put project files in. It's open source, you are able to make changes to all the files.

Just be sure not to change anyone elses projects :D
